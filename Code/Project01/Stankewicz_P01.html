How to make Easy Funfetti Cake Batter Dip Recipe

This delicious dip is a great snack for anyone! It is so easy and contains only three ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 box Funfetti Cake Mix
  • 2 cups of Cool Whip Lite (or regular)
  • 2 cups of Vanilla Yogurt
  • Sprinkle Decorations (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl mix cake mix, Cool Whip, and yogurt until blended and smooth.
  2. Chill for at least 2 hours.
  3. Transfer to serving bowl and top with addtitional sprinkles
  4. Serve with vanilla wafers, graham crackers, animal crackers, etc.
  5. Enjoy your fabolous dip!

Prep time: 5 minutes
Total time: 5 minutes
